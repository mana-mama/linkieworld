About

MANA MAMA is a design-based toy manufacturer from Thailand. We believe that good toys should be accessible to children worldwide, particularly those in need.

We specialize in producing educational materials, including coding and robotics toys, building toys, and educational toys for school supplies, primarily for the domestic market, and for more than 10 countries worldwide.

In 2013, our company received the Thailand Trusted Mark (TTM) from the Department of International Trade Promotion, Ministry of Commerce, Thailand. Our products conform to international toy safety standards.

Our original LINKIE product made its debut to the world at the Spielwarenmesse in 2015 ​

We welcome collaboration with publishers, developers, educational institutions, and organizations for custom projects.
