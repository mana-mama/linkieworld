linkieworld

platform toys for creative idea!

LINKIEWORLD is a platform for toys that inspire creative ideas. With a focus on "Dot Line Imagination," the platform encourages learning through basic shapes and methods.

Whether you're a child or adult, learning never ends, and LINKIEWORLD aims to help you find new and creative ways to evoke new ideas.

The LINKIE FAMILY

The "LINKIE FAMILY" is characterized by the use of "dots" and "lines" as a signature design.
This design element is present across all LINKIE product lines, making it easy to play and create with.

LINKIE, LINKIEMINI, and LINKIECODE are all designed to be fun and engaging for children of all ages.
